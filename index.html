Importances of OPEN RESTAURANTS INSPECTIONS

Restaurant inspections in New York City are crucial for protecting public health by enforcing safety regulations, preventing foodborne illnesses, and ensuring transparency through a public letter grading system. Inspections, which occur at least annually, check for proper food handling, temperature control, and pest management, with inspectors authorized to close establishments that pose immediate risks
